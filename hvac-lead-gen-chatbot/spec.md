# HVAC Lead Gen Chatbot Demo - Specification

## Overview
AI-powered chatbot for HVAC businesses to capture, nurture, and convert leads through websites and social platforms.

## Core Features

### 1. Local SEO Optimization
- **Google Business Profile integration**
  - Display business name, address, phone (NAP)
  - Show service areas
  - Enable review prompts
  - Display operating hours
  - Share special offers/promotions

### 2. Targeted Service Ads
- **PPC-style targeting**
  - Maintenance service ads (with pricing & location)
  - Repair service ads
  - Installation quotes
  - Emergency service hotlines

### 3. Social Media Integration
- **Multi-platform presence**
  - Facebook Messenger
  - WhatsApp Business
  - Telegram
  - Instagram DMs

### 4. Educational Content Hub
- **How-to video library**
  - Filter: Air conditioner troubleshooting
  - Filter: Thermostat installation
  - Filter: Furnace maintenance
  - Filter: Vent cleaning

### 5. Review Management
- **Social proof collection**
  - Automated review requests
  - Review display on chat
  - Respond to customer feedback
  - Google review prompts

### 6. Landing Page Optimizer
- **Conversion-focused design**
  - Clear CTAs
  - Service highlights
  - Trust badges
  - Emergency contact info

### 7. Expertise Content
- **Blog/integration system**
  - HVAC maintenance tips
  - Industry news
  - Product guides
  - Seasonal preparedness

### 8. Easy Booking
- **Online scheduling**
  - Calendar sync
  - Availability display
  - Service selection
  - Technician assignment

### 9. Email Retargeting
- **Timely email campaigns**
  - Seasonal reminders
  - Discount offers
  - Job confirmations
  - Maintenance schedules

## Technical Requirements

### AI Model
- **Provider:** Anthropic Claude (sonnet-4-6)
- **Knowledge Base:** HVAC industry training data
- **Persona:** Professional, friendly, HVAC-savvy

### Integration Points
- [ ] CRM API (HubSpot/Pipedrive)
- [ ] Google Business Profile
- [ ] Facebook Pixel
- [ ] Email marketing platform
- [ ] Scheduling calendar (Google/Outlook)

### Lead Scoring
- **High priority:** Emergency + recent search + location match
- **Medium priority:** Service request + business hours check
- **Low priority:** Informational queries + content browsing

## Lead Quality Tiers

### Tier 1: Emergency
- AC/Furnace failure
- Nighttime calls
- 5+ star previous jobs
- **Action:** Immediate call + dispatch

### Tier 2: High Intent
- Repair quotes
- Installation inquiries
- Maintenance reminders
- **Action:** Same-day callback + booking link

### Tier 3: Nurturing
- General questions
- Content browsing
- **Action:** Email sequence + content delivery

## Revenue Model

### Service Packages
- **Starter:** Chatbot + Lead capture - $750/mo
- **Growth:** +CRM + Email auto - $1,250/mo
- **Enterprise:** +Analytics + Priority - $1,800/mo

### Target ROI
- Cost per lead: $25-50
- Conversion rate: 15-25%
- Average job value: $500-1,500
